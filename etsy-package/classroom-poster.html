Recording Rules

1
🤫

Quiet on Set!

When you see the red light, recording is live. Keep background noise down and respect the creator.

2
📋

Plan Before You Press REC

Write down what you want to show. Use the Teleprompter to keep your script visible while recording.

3
🎯

Keep It Short & Fun

The best tutorials are under 5 minutes. One idea per video. Use markers to create chapters!

4
🧪

Test Your Setup First

Run a Mic Check before recording. Make sure your screen and camera are visible in the scene.

5
🙅

No Personal Info On Screen

Close tabs with passwords, emails, or personal data before sharing your screen. Privacy first!

6
🤝

Share & Help Others

Export your tutorial, create a QR code, and share with your classmates. Teaching others is the best way to learn!

7
🏆

Earn Your Badges!

Complete daily challenges, earn XP, unlock achievements. Every recording makes you a better creator!

You are the teacher. The world is your classroom.
