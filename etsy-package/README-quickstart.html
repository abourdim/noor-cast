Getting Started

1
Open index.html in Chrome or Edge (no server needed)
2
Click + Screen to capture your screen, + Camera for webcam
3
Pick a scene layout (Full, Split, PiP, Side-by-Side, Focus, Pilot)
4
Press R to record — 3-2-1 countdown — you're live!
5
Press R again to stop — download your video from the Take Panel

Privacy & Security

100% Local — Zero Data Collection

NoorCast runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking, no analytics. All recordings and settings are stored on your device. Perfect for schools with strict privacy policies.

Languages

Built-in support for English, French, and Arabic (with full RTL support). Switch languages in Settings.
