NoorCast

Multi-Camera Screen Recorder for Teachers
Zero install • 100% local • Privacy-first • Kid-friendly
1
🌐

Open in Browser

Open index.html in Chrome or Edge. No server needed. Works offline!

2
📷

Add Sources

Click + Screen or + Camera to add your screen capture and webcam feeds.

3
🎬

Pick a Scene

Choose from 6 built-in layouts: Full, Side-by-Side, Picture-in-Picture, Split, Focus, or Pilot.

4
🔴

Record!

Press R or click the REC button. 3-2-1 countdown and you're live!

5
🎉

Save & Share

Stop recording to see the Take Panel. Download your video (WebM), generate a thumbnail, export a transcript, create a flyer, or share via QR code. All locally on your machine!
